About us

Urban Haven Apartments...

At Urban Haven Apartments, we've been offering comfortable and stylish living spaces in the heart of Murang'a, Kiharu for over 12 years. We cater to a variety of needs with our selection of single rooms, bedsitters, studio apartments, one and two-bedroom options, along with self-contained units for those seeking extra privacy.
